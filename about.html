About AG Biology

Welcome to AG BIOLOGY! Our YouTube channel is dedicated to exploring the diverse branches of biology, including molecular biology, biochemistry, microbiology, and evolution.

In addition to our informative videos, we also prepare students for competitive exams such as CSIR NET Life Sciences, GATE XL, and Kerala PSC Biology exams. As your tutor, I am Aravind Gopi, a qualified CSIR JRF AIR 76 and a graduate of IISER Thiruvananthapuram. With my expertise and experience, I am committed to helping you achieve your academic and career goals in the field of biology.

Join us on AG BIOLOGY and let's explore the fascinating world of biology together! Spread the word if you like our content.
